What you get

Hands‑on training that combines HVAC/R, electrical fundamentals, and plumbing basics—plus business and soft‑skills. Based in Puerto Rico, we prepare motivated adults, including transitioning veterans, to step into paid roles with real momentum.

  • • HVAC/R — residential & light commercial
  • • Electrical fundamentals (circuits, safety, meters)
  • • Plumbing basics for service & install contexts
  • • Customer service & business operations
  • • Toolkit + tablet for learning
  • • EPA cert prep, OSHA awareness & background check

* Tuition, stipends, housing, and tools reflect the program model. Confirm current details with Admissions.
